Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is a pasta dish made with eggs, hard cheese, fatty cured pork, and black pepper. The dish took its modern form and name in the middle of the 20th century.

Ingredients

For 2 people

  • 170g spaghetti
  • 50g parmesan
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 100g guancciale (or bacon)
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Put water with salt to boil
  2. In the meantime cut the guancciale in small pieces and put it on a hot pan to render the fat
  3. Once the guancciale is golden brown we set aside for later
  4. On a bowl, beat the egg yolks
  5. Add the cheese, a pinch of salt and black pepper to the yolks
  6. Once the water is boiling, cook the spaghetti to 'al dente'
  7. Add the yolks with the cheese to the pan with the guancciale, with the heat turned off
  8. When the spaghetti are cooked, add some cooking water to the sauce and stir thoroughly
  9. Immediately afterwards add the spaghetti to the pan and mix everything together
  10. Serve on a plate and top with a sprinkle of black pepper
