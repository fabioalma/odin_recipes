Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such as ragù, béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses, seasonings and spices.

Ingredients

  • 1kg of minced meat (a blend of 70:30 beef-pork)
  • 1 onion
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 branch of celery
  • 300ml red wine
  • 800g tomato sauce
  • 50ml milk
  • 3 tea spoons olive oil
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • pasta sheets
  • bechamel

Steps

Sauce:

  1. Mince the vegetables in small cubes
  2. Add a spurt of olive oil to a pan and heat it
  3. Add the veggies to the pan
  4. Add salt to liking
  5. Cook the veggies at medium fire until they soft
  6. Add the minced meat to the pan
  7. Turn the heat to high and stir vigorously
  8. Once the meat has changed color, add the red wine to the pan
  9. Cook until the alcohol has evaporated
  10. Add the tomato sauce
  11. Once it has started boiling, turn the heat to low and cover it leaving a gap for the vapor to come out
  12. Let the sauce cook for 2-4 hours. Stir it from time to time and be patient
  13. Add salt and pepper to your liking
  14. Add the milk to correct the acidity

Assembly of the lasagna:

  1. Butter down the baking tray you'll be using
  2. Cover the bottom with bechamel
  3. Add a layer of pasta sheets on top
  4. Cover it with the sauce
  5. Put bechamel on top of the sauce
  6. Repeat step 3 through 5 until you get to the top of your baking tray or you run out of sauce/pasta
  7. Add a final layer of bechamel on top and sprinkle parmesan cheese over it
  8. Bake at 180ºC or 360ºF for 45 minutes. The last 5 minutes use them to gratin the top cheese
  9. Let it rest 10 to 15 minutes before serving
